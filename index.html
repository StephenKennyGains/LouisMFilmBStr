Louis Maxwell Film

Give your creativity over to the world

Louis is a film-maker from Dublin. He has spent the past five years working as a freelance artist creating pieces for clients such as The Department of Local Government, Housing and Heritage, Áras an Uachtaráin, Unislim LTD, The World Meeting of Families and many more. He received a first class honours in cinematography from the National Film School of Ireland. As a film-maker Louis’ goal is to bring the clients vision to life in a creative and innovative way.
